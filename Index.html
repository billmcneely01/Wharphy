FAQ

Quick answers merchants ask before launching same-day delivery.

What areas do you serve?

Wharphy can be configured by service zones. Add your city/metro coverage here and expand as volume grows.

Do customers get tracking?

Yes—customers receive real-time status and ETA updates, plus proof of delivery when complete.

Can I schedule daily pickups?

Absolutely. Set one or more pickup windows per day based on your fulfillment flow and cutoff times.

What proof of delivery is available?

Photo proof is standard. Signature capture can be added for higher-value deliveries or regulated items.
